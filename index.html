Chilean, Entrepreneur & Tech Visionary
English | Spanish

Diego Stevens is a visionary tech entrepreneur, strategic advisor, and founder of high-impact companies that scale from Latin America to the world. With over a decade of experience working across corporate innovation, startup ecosystems, and public-private partnerships, he brings a unique ability to connect business strategy, human performance, and technology. As the founder of Cleverit and Meetcard, Diego has led digital transformation for organizations through disruptive software, SaaS models, and AI adoption. He currently advises startups and C-level executives on how to scale purpose-driven businesses, raise capital, and create bold, sustainable impact. Whether advising on company boards, investing in early-stage ventures, or leading cultural change within teams, Diego’s approach blends commercial acumen with a deep commitment to human development. His work is powered by a strong team, visionary thinking, and a track record of transforming ideas into real-world innovation.
